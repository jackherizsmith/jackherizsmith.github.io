My application for Founders and Coders

Hey, I'm Jack. Here's my face. Thank you so much for visiting my first ever webpage, which pulls together all the components of the rest of my application to join the Founders and Coders Spring 2020 cohort - it's a real pleasure to have you here.

I've video'd myself saying a few words about the process of putting this site together, my experience with the prerequisites and also generally how enthusiastic I am about the work you do, below. I am so excited to be putting myself forward to learn with and play an active part in your awesome community of developers.

Very quick background: I studied engineering at university during which time I did a bit of C++ coding - I loved it. When I graduated in 2013 it was to pursue a career in partnership fundraising, first for Great Ormond Street and now with Shelter. You can see more details at my LinkedIn in the navigation above, and you could read through my recommendations there to get a sense of how I naturally find opportunities to collaborate with teams I'm a part of.

I'm applying to join your programme because I believe in the power of technology to make the world a better place, and after lots of research and meeting with past and current cohortees, it is clear that Founders and Coders is the coding bootcamp that is offering a route to having the biggest social impact, whilst also encouraging and enabling its members to give back to its community.

Why am I applying?

Community

  • Working with ambitious and diverse coders

  • Opportunity to teach others during & after course

  • Alumni network & events

Course

  • Tech for Better - this is exactly what I plan to do long-term

  • Focus on pair programming

  • Opportunity to make an impact with meaningful projects

Career

  • Ideal launchpad for building my career in charity / social enterprise

  • An invaluable network of socially conscious coders

  • A proven track record of successful graduates
